Crop Portal provident for Good Choice

  • Small and marginal farmers in India face challenges such as low productivity, unpredictable weather, poor access to market information, and lack of scientific guidance.
  • Our proposed SmartCrop Advisory System provides real-time, low-cost, and personalized crop advisory using weather forecasting, soil information, and AI-based recommendations.
  • The system guides farmers on crop selection, sowing, fertilizer use, and weather precautions, with regional language and offlinesupport.
